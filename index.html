Hey, I'm a Year 2 Computer Science student at the National University of Singapore. I am passionate about design, especially digital art and UI/UX, because it brings colour to the black-and-white world of computer science. It's where I build an emotional connection to what I make. I love design as I can bring a sense of joy and curiosity into people's lives and share pieces of myself that feel honest, even if they are quiet and small.

I made this website as a design experiment to see if I could bring my vision to life, even without experience in web design. I wanted it to show a kind of care for detail, which we often find in art.

The dotted gradients give a layer of texture, like grains of a painting. At the same time, I kept the solid colours typically used in flat UI design to keep the experience familiar and comfortable for users. The result sits between flat design and skeuomorphism, landing on a style that is simple, but with enough texture to give it a crafted feel. I hope you will find this space refreshing yet familiar!
